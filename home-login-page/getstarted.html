SKILL SWAP NETWORK

The skill swap hub is an innovative and transformative solution aimed at revolutionizing the way students learn and support one another. In today's educational landscape, students often encounter challenges when seeking assistance and clarifying doubts in specific subjects. Moreover, students possess diverse strengths and weaknesses across various topics, creating an untapped potential for peer-to-peer knowledge exchange. The skill swap hub addresses this gap by providing a tailored platform that intelligently matches students based on their individual proficiency levels and strengths.
